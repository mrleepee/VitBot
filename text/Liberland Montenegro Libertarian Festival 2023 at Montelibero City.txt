SUMMARY
Vít Jedlička speaks at the 2023 Montelibero Libertarian Festival in Montenegro, shortly after Liberland gained physical access to its territory for the first time in 8 years (August 6, 2023). He describes the recent Croatian police raids and destruction of Liberland property, the blockchain-based governance and justice system using Liberland merits as collateral, ongoing construction projects including a presidential palace and marina, diplomatic achievements, the metaverse land register, and plans for a hotel and children's park. The talk includes a Q&A covering passport travel utility, border control, Croatian relations, and why starting a new country is preferable to reforming existing ones.

KEY POINTS
- First presentation after physically settling Liberland; gained access August 6, 2023 after 8 years of being denied entry
- Three factors enabled access: Croatia joining Schengen (lost border sovereignty), viral video of activist beaten planting flag (9M views), and personal meeting with Croatian president in Zagreb
- Croatian police conducted raids after 1.5 months, destroying ~60,000 EUR of property including security house, stealing equipment including Starlink
- Blockchain governance: all institutions (Congress, Senate, courts, registries, budgeting) on one proof-of-stake platform; Liberland dollar works like Bitcoin
- Merit system: shares of Liberland that serve as voting power, land rights (10 merits = 1 sq meter), and justice collateral; wrongdoers can lose staked merits to compensate victims
- Inspired by Switzerland, Liechtenstein, Singapore but with completely voluntary taxes (only land fee allowed)
- Legislation under 200 pages; 4 ministries; elections every 3 months; 21 Congress members; decentralized judiciary
- 700,000+ citizenship applicants; interest went 10x after territorial settlement; e-residency costs $150
- Infrastructure: marina, presidential palace under construction, airport planned, farm, horse area, Liberty Island
- Diplomatic presence worldwide: embassy in Washington DC (from Brock Pierce), connections to El Salvador and Bitcoin City
- 3D metaverse land register; metaverse designer became mayor of Liberland
- Projects: 10M EUR hotel, children's park, ARK Village in Serbia, resorts around Liberland
- Passport works in ~20% of world: most of Africa, Pacific Islands; recognized by exchanges like Kraken
- 24/7 security guards checking documents; visa system with merit-based fees planned
- Q&A: Jedlička is "president in exile" (kicked out of Croatia); finance minister lives permanently in Liberland; believes starting new country is better than reforming existing ones
